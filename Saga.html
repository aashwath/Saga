What the heck is it about?

Well, how best to describe it? Let’s go with “Star Wars acting like Game of Thrones with a dash of inspiration from The Jetsons (and a little help from Picasso)”? Yeah, let’s go with that.

Saga is a monthly comic written by Brian K. Vaughn (Y: The Last Man) and Fiona Staples (T.H.U.N.D.E.R. Agents, Archie), published by Image Comics. And it has captured the of attention whole galaxy.

It depicts a husband and wife, Alana and Marko, from long-warring extraterrestrial races, fleeing authorities from both sides of a galactic war as they struggle to care for their daughter, Hazel, who is born in the beginning of the series and who occasionally narrates the series as an unseen adult
